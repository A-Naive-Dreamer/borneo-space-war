Tutorial

  1. Players Movements

    Use a for move Player 1 to right and d to left. Otherwise use ˂ for move Player 2 to right and ˃ to left.

  2. Players Shooting

    Use w for Player 1 shooting and ˄ for Player 2 shooting.

  3. Game Over

    Game over if all enemy have defeated, one or more player have defeated by other player shot, or timer is already timeout.

  4. Exception

    If one player have defeated by enemies shot, game still run.
